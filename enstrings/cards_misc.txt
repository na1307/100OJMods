< CARD_TERM_UPGRADE >
UPGRADE: Instead of playing the card, the Upgrade cost can be played to turn this card into its upgraded version. The Upgrade uses the card use action of Standby phase.

< CARD_TERM_HYPER >
HYPER: Each unit has a unique Hyper card. By default, 8 blank Hyper cards are added to the Deck at the start of the game. Once drawn or otherwise acquired, a blank Hyper card becomes the Hyper card of the unit who drew it, until it is played or discarded and the deck is reshuffled.

< CARD_TERM_BATTLE >
BATTLE: Cards of this type can be used at the start of battle to apply effects.

< CARD_TERM_EVENT >
EVENT: Cards of this type affect all players or the field when played.

< CARD_TERM_BOOST >
BOOST: Cards of this type affect the user, or targetted player when played.

< CARD_TERM_GIFT >
GIFT: Cards of this type affect the holder when held.

< CARD_TERM_CONTRACT >
CONTRACT: Contract cards are held in a separate hand space in Bounty Hunt. They can be delivery quests, quests to hunt a particular Bounty monster, or a player. Completing contracts grants the amount of Fame printed on the contract.
